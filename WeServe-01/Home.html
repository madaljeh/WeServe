HOUSE SERVICES

A service booking site has been built consisting of a user and a service provider. A booking process that guarantees the ease of communication via phone or e-mail, dealing with the site and registration. So, this helps to do all things without any difficulties.

Read More

We were facing problems that occur at home and how to fix them by looking for a specialist to solve the problem or searching on social media sites for people who specialize in solving household problems. When it comes to the individual fixing the problem by themselves this might make the problem double and end up by not solving it. From my conclusion and my experience and searching for solutions to this problem, I found that we collect service providers on the website. It is easy for the user who faces the problem to communicate directly or book a specific service at a specific time and date, which facilitates saving time and effort. The site consists of two users, the service provider and the customer. They both are provided the ease of communication by phone or e-mail in booking the service and the process of booking the service by specifying the problem, time, date, and details of the problem.
